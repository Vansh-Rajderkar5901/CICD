The Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is one of the most prolific batsmen in the history of cricket. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, he has captained the Indian national team in all formats and is known for his aggressive batting style and leadership. Kohli has broken numerous records and is often called the "King Kohli" by fans worldwide.

Achievements

  • Most runs in T20 Internationals (over 4,000 runs)
  • Fastest to 10,000 ODI runs
  • Captain of India in multiple World Cups
  • ICC Cricketer of the Year (2017, 2018)
  • Multiple IPL titles with Royal Challengers Bangalore
